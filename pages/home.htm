Just who is Matthew Dooley?

With just twenty years of age, Matthew Dooley has spent most his life being raised in Yutan, Nebraska, a small town west of Omaha. Having graduated Yutan High School in 2015, Matthew has gone on to study computer science at the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO).

While attending UNO, Matthew has gotten involved in the student government agencies. He is currently a board member for UNO's Queer and Trans Services. Matthew is also one of two conference chairs for MBLGTACC 2018. MBLGTACC, the Midwest Bisexual Lesbian Gay Transgender Ally College Conference, is the largest LGBTQIA+ college conference in the United States of America.

But Matthew's involvment at UNO didn't justend in unpaid possitions. He was also a Technical Support Representative for the unviersity's Information Technology Services' Helpdesk until November of 2017. Matthew owns his own busines, Matthew Dooley Technologies, where he provides services in software development and techincal consulting. In the little free time left, Matthew enjoys many different sorts of activities. This includes everything from having a bonfire with some friends to taking an active role in politics.

To truly understand who Matthew is, you just have to meet him. You may gain a unique insight to who he is by following him on his various forms of social media.
